Website Accessibility and Usability Evaluation

Website URL

https://www.dratings.com

Website Name

Donchess Sports Computer Ratings and Predictions

Target Audience

The target audience appears to be sports enthusiasts, bettors, and individuals interested in sports predictions and ratings.

Site Organization

The site is organized with sections dedicated to upcoming games, sports ratings, and predictions. It aims to present sports data and analysis in a structured, easy-to-navigate format with quick access to current sports information and ratings.

CRAP Design Principles

**Contrast** is used to some extent in separating sections, with darker backgrounds behind text. However, the accessibility audit indicates a need to improve contrast to meet WCAG standards, as this was flagged as an issue in the audit report.

Audit Score

According to the accessibility checker, the site's audit score is 75, indicating non-compliance with certain accessibility standards and a potential risk of accessibility lawsuits.

Site Effectiveness

The site is somewhat effective in supporting users to complete actions related to sports ratings and predictions. However, the low accessibility score suggests that some users may face challenges in accessing content accurately due to issues such as low contrast.

Site Efficiency

For users familiar with sports data, the site is likely efficient, allowing quick access to ratings and predictions. However, accessibility issues could slow down task completion for users with disabilities or those unfamiliar with the layout.

Site Engagement

The site is somewhat engaging for its target audience, as it offers up-to-date sports information. However, the usability could be improved to ensure a more pleasant experience for all users, particularly those who rely on accessible web design.

Recommendation for Improvement

To improve accessibility and compliance, the website should address the contrast issue identified in the audit. Using higher-contrast colors for text and backgrounds would improve readability for all users, including those with visual impairments. Implementing a more user-friendly navigation structure, with clear headings and labels, could also enhance both accessibility and overall user experience.
